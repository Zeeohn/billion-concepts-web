About Us

Billion Concepts International Limited was incorporated as a Private Limited Liability Company under the Companies and Allied Matters Decree of 1990. The company has its corporate offices located at Legion House, 9 Kaura Namoda close, Area 3, Garki - Abuja and Paper House, Plot 445, Behind Living Faith Church by Commerce plaza, Durumi, Abuja. It commenced operation immediately after its incorporation and obtaining the necessary permits, licenses, and authorities to do business.

We are a multi-faceted company that provides comprehensive services on Designing, Branding, Printing & Publishing, Building Technology, Engineering, Procurement and Consultancy. We also offer advisory services on company branding and provide programs for developing corporate companies.

We have a pool of experienced professionals who have engaged in many large scale projects, mostly involved in the production and operation of corporate businesses. We have Coren certified Engineers, Architects, Quantity Surveyors, Building Technologies, certified professional Printers, certified Graphic designers, Consultants and various specialists who are reliable, versatile and very efficient with top-notch solutions to client's unique demands.
